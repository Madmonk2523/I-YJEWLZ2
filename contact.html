Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn items in their original packaging. Items must be returned with all original documentation and certificates.

Do you offer custom jewelry design?

Yes! Our expert designers can work with you to create a custom piece that's uniquely yours. Contact us to schedule a consultation.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (2-3 days) and overnight options are also available.

Are your diamonds certified?

Absolutely! All our diamonds come with certification from reputable gemological laboratories, ensuring authenticity and quality.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer flexible financing options for purchases over $1,000. Apply at checkout or contact us for more information.

Can I get my ring resized?

We offer free ring resizing within the first year of purchase. Additional resizing services are available for a nominal fee.
